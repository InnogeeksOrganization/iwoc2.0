About IWOC 1.0

• Glimpses of IWOC 1.0, organized by Innogeeks!
In this month-long Open Source Contribution event held in February 2023, we were excited to have facilitated 30+ impactful projects, hosted 4 engaging speaker sessions comprising of speaker like Unnati Chhabra - SDE @zomato, Shashank Srivastava - India Business @Google, and others with garnered registrations from a 350+ students, and actively involved 150+ contributors.
Through our unique approach, maintainers were felicitated for acquiring valuable contributions on their projects, resulting in the successful merging of 100+ PRs.
With the collaborative effort of 30+ maintainers and support from 20 mentors, we created a vibrant ecosystem of learning and innovation.
Our Discord server saw participation from 300+ members, creating a supportive and enriching experience for new contributors in the exciting world of open source.

Frequently asked questions

How many team members can I invite?

It is an individual event.

Do i need to be good in development for taking part?

No! You can be a beginner and still contribute, we're there to help you out.

How to contribute?

The project links are availabe on the site. One can use their github id for making the contributions

Is the event online/offline?

Hybrid. Speaker Sessions will be both online and offline.

Can I participate in IWOC both as a maintainer and a Contributor?

Yes, you can participate as Both.

Are participants from other colleges allowed to participate in IWoC 2024?
